Importance of NYC Parks
New York City Parks is important because it conserves nature in an very industrailized city and gives people a place to relax. In addition, it is home to many animals such as squirrels.Having parks also improves the water quality, protects groundwater, improves the quality of air, and much more. Although we don't think about the benefits of a park can have in our life, it is very important to maintaining healthy living conditions for people.

Animals that live in NYC Parks
When you visit a NYC park, you should expect to see some wildlife animals in the park as well. The animals that you should expect to see is squirrels, turtles, pigeons, and butterflies. However, there's other animals that you may have not thought of seeing in the NYC park such as bats, coyotes, raccoons that also live in New York City but it depends on the park you go. If you go upstate you more likely to see wild life than being in the city of New York.
