Dominican Republic


The Dominican Republic is the second largest caribbean country. The country is known as hispaniola.This name was given to the island by the colonizer, Christopher Colombus. Tainos of the island named the island Ayiti. Ayiti means flower of the high land. The other side of the island known as Haiti kept the name the tainos named the island centuries ago. Due to colonizers that came to the island, they created a divide between dominicans and haitians that still exist today. With all this being said, the dominican republic is great place for hot climate, diverse people, and rich history.


Places to Visit

  • Boca Chica: A beach in the dominican republic. The main dish served is
    seafood but you can also get other foods that does not have to do with fish.
    The view of the beach is very beautiful with white sand and water as clear
    as a mirror.
  • Punta Cana: One of the famous beaches and resort in the dominican
    republic. Bavaro Beach is north of Punta Cana that is great for swimming
    turquoise water.
  • Guayacanes Beach: It is a fishing village that you see locals fishing in the
    beach. It is a great site to see.
